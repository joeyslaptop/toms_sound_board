Thomas's Sound Board — Info & Instructions

A D&D soundboard and quick dice roller for Dungeon Masters.

Site: ofthecats.com

Description

"Thomas's Sound Board" is a 30-pad soundboard created as a Christmas gift for my son, Thomas. Up to 30 custom sounds can be assigned/reassigned to the color-ordered pads. Then tap or click pads to trigger effects instantly. The left panel also includes a “Random Roll” generator. Set it to D-however many you want with a number range spanning from -9999 to 9999.

How to use the sound pads

  • Select a pad to assign a sound:
    • Mac: Right-click a pad.
    • iPhone/iPad: Press and hold the pad (long-press).
      When the pad has been selected, it will glow around the edges.
  • Load Sound: After selecting a pad, click Load Sound and select an audio file from your computer, device or cloud storage.
  • Record Sound: After selecting a pad, click Record Sound to record (up to ~15 seconds). Click the red button when you're done recordingp.
  • Play a sound: Click/tap a pad you have assigned a sound to.

Tip: Assigned pads show a subtle outline/glow and display the first 10 characters of the sound name.

Reassigning a pad

If a pad already has a sound assigned, and you want to change it to a different sound, select it again to reassign. Don't worry, it will warn you before it deletes the old sound. If you continue, the old cached sound is removed and the pad becomes available for a new sound. If you cancel during the process before you load a sound, the pad will remain unassigned.

Random Roll

  • Enter a min value and max value.
  • Press Random Roll.
  • The digital display shows the result.

Clear Board

Clear Board removes all saved sounds from this device/browser and resets pad assignments. It's like a fresh start, a clean slate, a new world. You get the point. Don't do it if you don't want all the sounds on the board to be cleared.

Note: Clearing your browser/site data will also remove cached sounds, since the board stores audio locally in the browser.

Mac instructions

  • Open https://ofthecats.com in Safari or Chrome.
  • Right-click a pad to select it.
  • Click Load Sound and choose an audio file.
  • Click a pad to play.

iPhone / iPad instructions

  • Open https://ofthecats.com in Safari.
  • Long-press a pad to select it.
  • Tap Load Sound and pick audio from the Files app.
  • Tap a pad to play.

Nice on iPad: In Safari, tap Share → Add to Home Screen to use it like an app.

Privacy

Your sound files are chosen from your device and are stored locally in your browser for convenience. They are not uploaded to the website automatically.

Troubleshooting

  • Sound won’t play: Try reloading the page.
  • Recording won’t work: Allow microphone access when prompted.
  • Lost sounds: Sounds can be lost if browser storage is cleared.
